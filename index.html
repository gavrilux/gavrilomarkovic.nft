Gavrilo Markovic

Gavrilo Markovic is a new film director who was born in Serbia and from an early age moved to Spain where he grew up. He was good at sports so decided to give basketball a try and succeed enough to play in LEB Oro in the second Spanish basketball league. An injury make him give a shot in front of the cameras as a model and as an actor. He loved the experience a lot and  pushed a career in that world as well doing some stuff as this music video with more than 400M views on Youtube. Now with all that background, in parallel, he's stepping up behind the cameras wanting to share the stories he thinks are worth sharing.
